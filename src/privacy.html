Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

🔒 Short version: When you use the calculator without signing in, all data stays on your device and we collect nothing. When you choose to sign in, only your calculator scenarios are stored in the cloud — no advertising, no profiling, no sharing.

👤1. Who We Are

The CCS Calculator is a free, open-source tool for Australian families to estimate their Child Care Subsidy (CCS) entitlements. It is not affiliated with the Australian Government, Services Australia, or the Department of Education.

For questions about this policy, open an issue on the GitHub repository.

📋2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Without Signing In (Default)

When you use the calculator without an account, we collect no data whatsoever.

  • All income figures, work-hour inputs, and child details exist only in your browser's local storage.
  • Nothing is transmitted to any server.
  • Clearing your browser data removes all stored calculator information.

2.2 When You Sign In (Optional)

Signing in is entirely optional and unlocks cross-device sync. When you choose to sign in, we store:

Data Item Why It Is Stored Where It Is Stored
OAuth user ID (anonymous hash) Identifies your account without revealing your identity Azure Table Storage
Email address Provided by your OAuth provider (Microsoft/GitHub) for display only Azure Table Storage
Calculator scenarios (income, hours, children) So you can access your data across devices Azure Table Storage (encrypted at rest)
App preferences (theme, period) Remember your display settings Azure Table Storage
Last sync timestamp Detect and resolve multi-device conflicts Azure Table Storage

We do not collect:

  • Your full name (unless your email contains it — we cannot control that)
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Device identifiers or browser fingerprints
  • Usage analytics, page views, or click tracking
  • Any financial account information

🎯3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the calculator service:

  • Sync: Load and save your calculator scenarios across your devices.
  • Preferences: Remember your display settings (theme, default period).
  • Authentication: Verify your identity so only you can access your scenarios.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, machine learning, or any purpose other than operating the calculator.

🤝4. Data Sharing

We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except:

  • Microsoft Azure — Cloud infrastructure provider that stores your encrypted scenario data. Microsoft's use of this data is governed by their Data Protection Agreement.
  • Your OAuth provider — Microsoft or GitHub authenticate your identity and share a user ID and email with us. Refer to their privacy policies for how they handle your credentials.
  • Legal requirements — We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

🕑5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you have not signed in for 12 months, your cloud data may be automatically purged.

You can delete your data at any time — see Section 7: How to Delete Your Data.

🔐6. Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
  • Data is encrypted at rest in Azure Table Storage.
  • Authentication uses industry-standard OAuth 2.0 — we never see or store your password.
  • Each user's data is isolated by a unique partition key (your user ID); other users cannot access your scenarios.
  • API endpoints require a valid authentication token on every request.
  • The source code is open-source and publicly auditable on GitHub.

🗑️7. How to Delete Your Data

7.1 Delete Local Browser Data

If you have never signed in, this removes all calculator data:

  1. Open the calculator at index.html.
  2. Scroll down to the Form Actions section.
  3. Click the "Clear Data" button to wipe all locally stored inputs.
  4. Alternatively, clear your browser's local storage via your browser's developer tools or privacy settings.

7.2 Delete Cloud Account Data

If you have signed in and want to delete all data stored in the cloud:

  1. Sign in to the calculator using your Microsoft or GitHub account.
  2. Open the account menu (top right — your name or "💾 Sign in").
  3. Click "Sign Out" to end your session.
  4. To request permanent deletion of all your cloud data, open an issue on GitHub titled "Data Deletion Request".
  5. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days of the request.

⚠️ Data deletion is permanent and irreversible. Your calculator scenarios cannot be recovered after deletion. Export your scenarios (CSV) before requesting deletion if you want to keep a copy.

7.3 Revoke OAuth Access

You can revoke the CCS Calculator's access to your account at any time without deleting your data:

  • Microsoft: account.microsoft.com/privacy/app-access
  • GitHub: github.com/settings/applications → Authorised OAuth Apps

⚖️8. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — Request permanent deletion of your data (see Section 7).
  • Portability — Export your calculator scenarios to CSV at any time using the Export button.
  • Objection — Opt out of cloud storage by signing out and using the calculator in anonymous mode.

To exercise any of these rights, open an issue on the GitHub repository.

🍪9. Cookies

The CCS Calculator uses no tracking cookies. The only browser storage used is:

  • localStorage — Saves your calculator inputs and preferences locally on your device. Never transmitted to a server unless you sign in.
  • Session cookies — Set by Azure Static Web Apps during authentication. These expire when you sign out or close the browser.

👶10. Children's Privacy

The calculator is designed for use by parents and guardians, not children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently done so, please contact us via GitHub.

📝11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be committed to the GitHub repository with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the calculator after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

✉️12. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Open an issue on GitHub
